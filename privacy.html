01. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Shubh Kothari ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit my personal portfolio website and interact with my digital services.

As a freelance web developer and creative technologist, I respect your privacy and am committed to maintaining a secure digital environment. This site operates with minimalism in mind, collecting only what is strictly necessary to provide a high-end experience and facilitate communication.

02. Data Collection

I intentionally limit the data collected through this digital experience. Information is generally categorized into two types:

  • Information you provide directly: When you reach out via email (shubhkothari.dev@gmail.com) or inquire about freelance services, I collect your name, email address, and the contents of your message.
  • Information collected automatically: To ensure the smooth running of WebGL animations and GSAP interactions, basic, non-personally identifiable technical data (such as browser type, device resolution, and general geographic region) may be temporarily processed by standard analytics or hosting providers.

03. Use of Information

Any information collected is used exclusively for professional purposes:

  • To maintain the security and integrity of this digital space.

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties under any circumstances.

04. Third-Party Integrations

This portfolio utilizes modern web technologies that may involve third-party services. These include standard content delivery networks (CDNs) to load libraries like Three.js and GSAP, as well as Google Fonts. These providers may receive your IP address as part of the standard HTTP request process necessary to deliver these assets to your browser.

05. Security & Retention

I implement reasonable security measures to protect any professional correspondence. Client inquiries and related data are retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by standard business practices.
